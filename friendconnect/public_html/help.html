How to Use FriendConnect

Logging In

Unless you've got an account created, you'll need to register a new one with FriendConnect! To do so, all fields under the Create Account section must be filled out. Additionally, the email address you provide must be in the following format: somestring@somestring.somestring! Accounts already associated with an email address cannot have another account associated with them! A log in session = 60 minutes! Another thing to note: by default, account creation makes your account PUBLIC.

Side Bars & Main Page

Once on the home page, there are 3 sections: the left side contains several buttons allowing for navigation across the site, the main portion contains the current information you are viewing (posts, users, groups, etc.); default is posts, and the right side contains information concerning your friends, the groups you are in, and pending friend requests for you to accept/decline.

Making Friends

The whole point of FriendConnect is to make new friends/meet new users! If you want to make friends or view other users on this site, click the View Other Profiles on the home page. This should lead you to a page where you can view other people's profiles, read their bios, and send, accept, and decline friend requests! Additionally, once you're friends with someone, you can unfriend them as well (if you so choose).

Posts & Comments

Once you're on the home page, it may seem pretty blank if you've got no friends making posts yet; however, to make a new post, click the Make a New Post button, which should redirect you to an interface that allows you to make a new text-based post. When you make a new post, it should redirect you to your feed and the post should be there! To comment on a post, simply click Comment and that should redirect you to another page where you can write your comment! Main posts can be edited, liked, and unliked as well! The default home page/feed includes posts visible to you (so your posts and the posts of your friends)!

Groups

Alongside making friends, you can also create groups! To make a new group, simply click the Make a New Group button, which should redirect you to an interface that allows you to create a new group with a name and a brief bio that describes what the group is about. To view other groups, you can click to View Groups button. Here, you are taken to a page where you can view other groups, their bios, and decide whether or not you'd like to join/leave groups!

Edit Profile

Unhappy with some of the information you entered? Or would you like to update your bio or privacy settings? Click the Edit Profile button on the home page. Here, you can modify your first and last name, your bio that is potentially displayed to other users! Additionally, you have the ability to make your account Public or Private (a private account hides your bio from other users)! You can also add/update your profile picture here to show other users who you are!

Logging Out

Ready to log out? Click the LOGOUT button on the upper right corner of the page and your current session should end!
